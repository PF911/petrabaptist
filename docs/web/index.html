About Petra Baptist Church

Our Mission

Petra Baptist Church is a community of believers dedicated to knowing Christ and making Him known. We are built on the solid rock of God's Word, committed to worship, fellowship, discipleship, and service.

What We Believe

We believe in the authority of Scripture, the deity of Christ, salvation by grace through faith, and the transforming power of the Holy Spirit. Our desire is to see lives changed by the Gospel and communities transformed by God's love.

Our History

Located in beautiful St. Augustine, Florida, Petra Baptist Church has been serving our community for years, providing a place where people can encounter God, grow in their faith, and serve others with joy.

Our Ministries

Opportunities to grow, serve, and connect

Adult Ministry

Bible studies, small groups, and fellowship opportunities for adults of all ages to grow deeper in their faith and build meaningful relationships.

Children's Ministry

Engaging programs that teach children about God's love through age-appropriate lessons, worship, and activities in a safe, nurturing environment.

Youth Ministry

Dynamic programs designed to help teens navigate life's challenges while building a strong foundation of faith and Christian community.

Worship Ministry

Leading our congregation in heartfelt worship through music, helping people encounter God's presence and express their devotion to Him.

Outreach Ministry

Serving our local community through various programs, demonstrating Christ's love through practical acts of service and compassion.

Prayer Ministry

Supporting our church family and community through intercessory prayer, believing in the power of God to transform lives and circumstances.
