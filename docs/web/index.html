About Petra Baptist Church

Our Mission

Petra Baptist Church is a community of believers dedicated to knowing Christ and making Him known. We are built on the solid rock of God's Word, committed to worship, fellowship, discipleship, and service.

What We Believe

We believe in the authority of Scripture, the deity of Christ, salvation by grace through faith, and the transforming power of the Holy Spirit. Our desire is to see lives changed by the Gospel and communities transformed by God's love.

Our History

Located in beautiful St. Augustine, Florida, Petra Baptist Church has been serving our community for years, providing a place where people can encounter God, grow in their faith, and serve others with joy.
